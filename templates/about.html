About

Our mission is to make information available to all.

Description

Kernle is an educational application to quickly identify the central/most important part (kernel) of a concept.

Why Kernle?

Problem

There is a ton of information on the internet. However, a lot of that information requires significant background knowledge and time to sift through. The wikipedia page on quantum physics is very complicated and hard to understand.

Kernle's Solution

This is where Kernle comes in. Kernle provides the central/most important part (kernel) of a concept. Understanding the central/most important part of a concept makes it much easier to then use the internet's vast knowledge to explore the details of the concept.

Use Cases

Use Kernle to learn, review, and teach.
